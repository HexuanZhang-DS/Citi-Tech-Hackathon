Benefits of Univest for Students

No job? No worries.

It’s a tough economy out there. We understand that. With Univest if you cant find a job after college you don’t pay a dime until you do.

It's a deal

Our investors make you offers. You choose the best one for you based on the percentage paid back and timeframe.

Rainy Day Fund

Investors sweeten the deal by offering you a rainy day fund. That way you always have money to fall back on in an emergency. Univest students are also preaproved for a Citi credit line.

We look out for you

We protect you against unfair deals. Never pay back more than 2.5x your tuition. Our minimum earnings protection means if you make less than $35k you don’t pay.
